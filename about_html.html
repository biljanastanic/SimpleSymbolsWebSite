Definiton

A symbol is something such as a particular mark that represents some piece of information. For example, a red octagon may be a symbol for "STOP". On a map, a picture of a table might represent a campsite. Numerals are symbols for numbers. All language consists of symbols. Personal names are symbols representing individuals.

Historical meaning

This history of a symbol is one of many factors in determining a particular symbol's apparent meaning. Consequently, symbols with emotive power carry problems analogous to false etymologies.
